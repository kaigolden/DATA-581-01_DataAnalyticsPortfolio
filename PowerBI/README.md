## What the Dashboard shows
A company's distribution of its 500 employees across departments and gender identification, along with a breakdown of employees that have resigned from the company. Attrition/resignation records show that 65 resignations, where apart from the senior level, most of these indiduals resigned voluntarily. This could be a useful starting point for employee satisfaction analysis. Across departments, Sales has the most employees, with 120 members, while HR has the fewest with 45 members. The company is male-dominant, with 284 of the company's 500 employees being male.<br><br>
These visualizations paint a picture of how the company's workforce is distributed across departments and suggests that, across a majority of its job levels, employees resign for voluntary reasons more often than not. These visuals could help the company identify where representation gaps exist and guide deeper exploration of employee satisfaction within each job level.
