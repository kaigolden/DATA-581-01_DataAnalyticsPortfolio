## Live Dashboard
[HR Analytics Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/kaili.golden/viz/HR_Analytics_Dashboard_17823347263180/Visuals_dash?publish=yes)

## What the Dashboard shows
A company's employee distribution of 500 people across their job level, department, and gender identification are shown. Mid-level employees with experience appear to be most prominent; the average age is 42 and the average years of tenure is 9, and the mid job level has the most employees with 138 people, with seniors having the second highest number of 116. The company's age distribution appears even with employees ranging from their twenties through their fifties before tapering off near the retirement age of sixties. Employees concentrate in sales with 120 members, and HR the fewest with 45 members. Gender breakdown shows that the company's workforce is male-dominant, with 284 being male, and 200 being female. The remaining 16 identify as non-binary, which may provide a starting point for representation analysis. <br>
Together, these visualizations answer what this company's workforce looks like. They may help to identify things such as representation gaps, job level balances, and more.
